## 🎬 PART 1 — "I Wasn't Supposed To Be Here"

### Hook (first 2 seconds — straight to camera, no intro)

I worked in pubs for 9 years.
I have no CS degree.
And I just built a full AI platform from scratch
in my house in Wales.

Let me tell you how that happened."

---

"In 2021 I built a Discord server.
Called it The BROski Clan.
I had zero audience.
Zero funding.
Just me, a laptop, and a brain that
wouldn't shut up at 2am."

"I taught myself bots. Auto-moderation.
Web3. Crypto tokens.

logged the transaction live in Discord.

Gas used: 0.000071546 Gwei.
I'll never forget that number."

*[pause — smile]*

"Nobody was watching.
I didn't care.
I was building."

## 🎬 PART 3 — "Why I'm Really Building This"
### The emotional closer — this is the one that goes viral

### Hook (quieter, more direct)

"I need to tell you the real reason
I'm building HyperFocus Zone."

*[pause]*

"It's not for clout.
It's not for money.
It's not even really for me."

---

"It's for the kid who sat in school
being told they were lazy.
Being told they just needed to try harder.
Being told the reason they couldn't
focus was a character flaw
and not a superpower pointing in
the wrong direction."

"That was me.
That's probably some of you watching this."

*[cut — show the platform]*

"So I built tools that work the way
our brains actually work.

In bursts.
In connections.
In hyperfocus sprints at 2am
when the rest of the world is asleep
and your brain is finally, finally
running at full speed."

*[cut — straight to camera]*

"HyperFocus Zone is open source.
It's free.
It's built by someone with dyslexia and ADHD
who was never supposed to be here.

And if you've got a brain like mine —
welcome home. 🐾"
