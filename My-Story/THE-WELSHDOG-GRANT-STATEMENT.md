# HyperFocus Zone — Founder Statement
**Lyndz (WelshDog) — June 2026 — Llanelli, Wales**

---

## Who I Am

My name is Lyndz. I'm a self-taught AI developer, platform builder, and entrepreneur from Llanelli, Wales.

I have dyslexia and ADHD.

I spent nine years working in hospitality before I taught myself to code. No degree. No bootcamp. No roadmap. Just a neurodivergent brain that found its thing and refused to stop.

---

## The Problem I'm Solving

Mainstream tech tools are not built for neurodivergent people.

The interfaces are overwhelming. The documentation assumes a linear brain. The education systems that teach coding are built for people who don't think the way I do — or the way 1 in 5 people in the UK do.

Dyslexia affects approximately 10% of the UK population. ADHD affects approximately 5%. Combined with other neurodivergent conditions, we're talking about millions of people who are underserved by every major tech education and productivity platform on the market.

---

## What I Built

Since 2021 I have built an open-source AI ecosystem called **HyperFocus Zone** — designed from the ground up for neurodivergent users.

The platform includes:

- **HyperCode-V2.4** — AI agent backend with local LLM support, multi-agent orchestration, and real-time observability
- **BROski-Obsidian-Brain** — A graph-powered knowledge system with 192 interconnected nodes (skills, code, notes) that thinks the way ADHD brains do — in connections, not lists
- **HYPER-SKILLS-Registry** — An agent skill catalogue enabling autonomous task routing
- **Hyper-Vibe-Coding-Course** — A neurodivergent-friendly coding education platform
- **The BROski Clan** — A thriving Discord community supporting neurodivergent creators and developers

Everything is open-source. Everything is free to access.

Wales has one of the highest rates of neurodivergent identification in the UK. The Welsh Government's commitment to digital inclusion and tech sector growth makes this the right place and the right time to build something that puts neurodivergent people at the centre of the AI revolution — not as an afterthought.

HyperFocus Zone is proof that a self-taught, neurodivergent developer from Llanelli can build world-class AI infrastructure.

Imagine what happens when we fund more people like me to do the same.
