##### 1.1. The Dual-Layered Brain

Listen up, look you, because this isn't some static note-taking setup—it’s a living digital nervous system. The BROski-Obsidian-Brain v3.0 architecture splits the load between two critical layers to keep your focus sharp and your system "autonomously alive."

We build for the ADHD, Autistic, and Dyslexic brain. No fluff, just results. These are the "Sacred Rules" of the Zone:

* **Productivity is the Soul of the Pet:**  Metadata (XP, energy) only shifts in response to real-world labor processed by the engine.  
* **Fast Feedback:**  Immediate dopamine-friendly rewards are mandatory to crush task paralysis.  
* **No Fluff Interactivity:**  Every interaction must provide utility, not just maintenance overhead.  
* **Absolute Ecosystem Sync:**  If the connection between the pet and the vault is severed, the pet loses its "brain." Ride or die, mun.  
* **Sensory Integrity:**  We use Level 12 CSS to eliminate visual noise and prevent executive dysfunction.

##### 1.3. The Docker Metropolis (HyperCode-V2.4)

The platform is a massive 32-container Docker metropolis. But pay attention to the  **Failure Chain** :  **Container \#30 (Hyper Brain)**  is the single point of failure. If any other container fails, you’ve just lost a "limb"—maybe your coding stats or a specific pet update. But if Container \#30 goes down, it’s a total brain shutdown. The vault reverts to a static pile of notes, and all AI-driven functionality—briefings, filtering, and splitting—goes dark.
