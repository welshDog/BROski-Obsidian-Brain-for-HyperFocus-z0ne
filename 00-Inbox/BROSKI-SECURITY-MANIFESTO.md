## WHO WE ARE

We are neurodivergent builders. ADHD. Dyslexic. Autistic. We think fast, trust easily, and build with heart. That's our superpower. But it also means we need a system that protects us and our users from people who want to exploit that kindness.

---

## WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

BROski is the world's first neurodivergent-first autonomous AI brain. It learns. It gamifies focus. It rewards progress. It connects a living second brain to a full AI ecosystem. It is not a toy. It is infrastructure for people who think differently.

---

## WHAT WE BELIEVE

- Security is not optional. It is a moral responsibility.
- Every user deserves to feel safe, especially those who are vulnerable.
- Power without protection is dangerous.
- A system that can be abused will be abused.
- We build for the kind people. So we must protect them fiercely.

---

## THE SECURITY PRINCIPLES

🔐 **Layers over locks** — No single password. Multiple layers always.

👥 **Multi-party approval** — Critical changes need more than one person.

⏱️ **Rotating codes** — Time-based one-time passwords. Never static.

🌊 **True randomness** — Quantum or environmental entropy for encryption keys.

📋 **Full audit trails** — Every action logged. Nothing hidden.

🛑 **Hard limits** — Economy and rewards cannot be gamed or exploited.

🔒 **Safe by default** — Locked down unless deliberately opened.

🧠 **Human oversight** — AI never acts alone on critical decisions.

---

## THE VISION

A platform so secure that even the most trusting, kind, and open-hearted neurodivergent user can build and create without fear. Where the walls are invisible but unbreakable. Where the fun never stops and the bad actors never get in.
