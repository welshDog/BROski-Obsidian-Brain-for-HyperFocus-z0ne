## 📊 Patterns to Watch

| Thing | Good Sign | Warning Sign |
|---|---|---|
| Sleep | 7+ hrs, consistent times | Under 5hrs, irregular |
| Food | 3 meals, protein | Skipping meals, sugar-only |
| Hydration | 6+ glasses | Headaches, dry mouth |
| Movement | Any daily movement | Days without moving |
| Meds | Consistent timing | Missed 2+ days |
| Screen breaks | Every 90 mins | 4hr+ marathon sessions |

## 🧠 Neurodivergent Notes

- **Hyperfocus sessions** burn more energy than you think — eat + drink DURING them
- **Task paralysis ≠ laziness** — if you can't start, the task is too big → use [[Hub/Focus-Command-Center|HyperSplit]]
- **Sensory overload** → switch CSS theme to 🌙 Calm mode (`Ctrl+Shift+F`)
- **Bad brain day** → do ONE tiny thing and call it a win
